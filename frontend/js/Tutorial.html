Quick Tutorial

Watch the short walkthrough and follow the steps below to start using Venture Magnate. You’ll learn how to register, log in, explore market data, place simulated trades (Market/Limit), and review your portfolio’s profit & loss.

Step 1

Register

Open Register, fill name, email, and password (confirm password). Your portfolio is created with a virtual balance.

Step 2

Login

Use your email and password. You’ll be redirected to the Portfolio dashboard.

Step 3

Explore Portfolio

See Cash, Equity, and P/L. Holdings table lists each symbol, quantity, average price, and unrealized P/L.

Step 4

Open Trading & Market Data

Search a symbol (AAPL, TSLA, BTCUSDT, EURUSD), choose Category (Stock/Crypto/Forex), pick Chart Type (Line/Candle/Depth).

Step 5

Place a Trade

Select Buy or Sell, choose Market (auto price) or Limit (enter price), set quantity, then Execute Trade.

Step 6

Review Results

Your cash, holdings, and transactions update instantly. Return to Portfolio to view P/L charts and history.

Need help? Start with symbols like AAPL (stocks), BTCUSDT (crypto), or EURUSD (forex). Prices may come from live APIs (Finnhub/Alpha Vantage/Binance) or cached data if rate-limited.
